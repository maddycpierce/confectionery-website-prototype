Who We Are
A Couple That Loves Candy

Beau's Bonbons is a candy store, created to celebrate and spread our love of candy. As husband and wife, we work side by side, with our goal being to produce the best candy that we can, for people of all ages to enjoy. Along with our team of expert candymakers, we design and create candies that are original and unique.
